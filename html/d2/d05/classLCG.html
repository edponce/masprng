int LCG::get_rn_int ( )
virtual

On machines with 32 bit integers, the Cray's 48 bit integer math is duplicated by breaking the problem into steps. The algorithm is linear congruential. M is the multiplier and S is the current seed. The 31 High order bits out of the 48 bits are returned.
